The Best Movies According to Chioma

My top 3 movies of all-time!

Crash Landing on you

This is my favourite movie as it encapsulates romance, action and the beauty of comaderie and friendship.

Empress Ki

It is a movie set in the historic times of the Joseon era now modern day Korea, which tells the tale of love, devotion and how people change in the face of power.

Lion King

Specifically the cartoon version produced in the 90s, this movie tugged at my hearstrings as a child as i came to understand the grief that comes with the loss of a loved one, betrayal by family and the power of bravery.
